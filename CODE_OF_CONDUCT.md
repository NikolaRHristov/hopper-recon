# Code of Conduct

This project adopts the **Contributor Covenant v2.1** without modification.
The full text is at <https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/>.

## Reporting

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be
reported to **iksnerd@users.noreply.github.com**. Reports are reviewed promptly and kept
confidential. Action — including warnings, temporary bans, or permanent bans
from project spaces — is taken at the maintainers' discretion per the
Covenant's [Enforcement Guidelines](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/#enforcement-guidelines).

Translations of the Covenant are available at
<https://www.contributor-covenant.org/translations>.
